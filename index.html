School for Global Leaders

Learn to Change the World.

Rigor - Leadership - Community

Located on the Lower East Side, School for Global Leaders is a middle school that redefining education. Students are immersed in rigorous academics, while their social development is fostered through community building courses.

School for Global Leaders offers a variety of programs including:

  • Integrated Projects Week
  • Advisory Programs
  • SONYC After School
  • Regents Courses
  • Boys' Sports: Football, Basketball, Baseball
  • Girls' Sports: Volleyball, Basketball, Softball
  • Drama
